California Privacy Notice

Notice for California Residents

Version | Effective date:

This notice supplements the NomNom Privacy Policy and applies to California residents under the California Consumer Privacy Act as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act.

1. Categories of Personal Information Collected

  • Identifiers and account information.
  • Commercial information and transaction records.
  • Internet or app activity information.
  • Geolocation data needed for delivery operations.
  • Customer support and communications records.

2. Business and Commercial Purposes

  • Service fulfillment, rider matching, and delivery operations.
  • Fraud prevention, safety, and legal compliance.
  • Analytics, troubleshooting, and platform improvement.

3. Your Rights

  • Right to know what personal information we collect and disclose.
  • Right to request deletion of personal information (subject to exceptions).
  • Right to request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to opt out of sale or sharing, if applicable.
  • Right to limit use of sensitive personal information, if applicable.
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights.

4. Submitting Requests

To submit a verifiable consumer request, email hello@campusventures.us with "California Privacy Request" in the subject line. We may request verification details before completing a request.

5. Authorized Agents

You may designate an authorized agent to submit a request on your behalf, subject to legal proof of authority and identity verification.

6. Data Retention

We retain categories of personal information only for as long as necessary for disclosed purposes, legal obligations, and safety investigations.

7. Metrics and Updates

We may publish updated rights-request methods and metrics as required by applicable law and regulations.
